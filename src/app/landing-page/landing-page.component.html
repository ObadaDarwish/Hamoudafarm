About us
Hamouda farms is a family business that started its operation in Egypt in 1985.
We are located in El Sadat city Cairo-Alexandria desert road.
As a part of our strategy to be one of the leading farms that exports fruits from the Egyptian market, in addition to planting different types of organic fruits and vegetables .
In 1985 we started by planting 4 acre now we reached 100 acre .
Furthermore our organic fruits are under the supervision of experienced engineers.
We use natural substances in order to overcome diseases and insects in the field and natural manures to improve fertility and insure nutrient-rich soil.
For any quality criteria – size, color, consistency, taste, shelf-life, and humidity retention – we seek the highest standards in the industry.
